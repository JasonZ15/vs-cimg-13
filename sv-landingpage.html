The Global Mobile Internet Conference (GMIC) is now coming to Silicon Valley. Join thousands of entrepreneurs, executives, investors, and developers this October at GMIC SV.

Learn from and network with key players in the world’s largest and hottest mobile markets. GMIC SV follows on the success of GMIC Beijing, Asia’s largest mobile Internet conference drawing over 5,200+ attendees from 32 countries in 2012.
